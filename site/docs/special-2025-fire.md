# Mourning the Wang Fuk Court Fire in Tai Po, Hong Kong (2025)

In memory of the tragic fire that occurred in Wang Fuk Court, Tai Po, Hong Kong, in 2025, we honour the lives lost and the resilience of the community affected by this devastating event. Our thoughts are with the families and friends of the victims during this difficult time.

In the meantime, the website will be displayed in grayscale. If this mode affects your accessibility, you may click on the button in the message to disable it.

The mourning message and grayscale filter will be displayed until at least 7th December 2025.

天佑大埔，天佑香港。 God bless Tai Po, God bless Hong Kong.
